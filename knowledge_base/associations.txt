#Student Cooperatives and Associations at Metropolia UAS

Student Union METKA
- Official student union of Metropolia UAS.
- Represents students’ rights and interests in university governance.
- Organizes events, parties, cultural activities, and sports.
- Provides student tutoring and peer support.
- Membership includes access to Zone Sports Services and student discounts.

Student Associations
- Each degree program has its own student association (ainejärjestö).
- Associations organize program-specific events, excursions, and networking opportunities.
- They provide peer support and represent students in faculty-level discussions.
- Examples:
  - Kultti ry (Culture and Arts students).
  - Insinööriopiskelijat (Engineering students).
  - Social Services and Health Care student associations.

Cooperatives and Project Groups
- Students can join or create cooperatives to work on real-life projects.
- Cooperatives often collaborate with companies, NGOs, and city organizations.
- They provide opportunities for entrepreneurship and innovation.
- Examples: 
  - Media and design cooperatives at Arabia Campus.
  - Tech and startup incubators at Karamalmi Campus.

Clubs and Communities
- Hobby clubs: music bands, theater groups, gaming and esports clubs.
- International student groups: support for exchange and degree students from abroad.
- Language and culture clubs: promote multicultural exchange.
- Volunteer groups: organize charity events and community projects.

Networking and Career Support
- Student associations collaborate with companies for internships and job fairs.
- Alumni networks provide mentoring and career guidance.
- Cooperatives often serve as bridges to employment after graduation.

Summary
Student cooperatives and associations at Metropolia UAS create a vibrant community where students can engage in academic, cultural, social, and entrepreneurial activities. They strengthen student voice, provide support, and open pathways to professional opportunities.
