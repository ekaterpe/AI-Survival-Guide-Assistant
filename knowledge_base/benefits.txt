Student Benefits, Discounts and Services in Helsinki (Metropolia UAS)

HSL (Helsinki Regional Transport)
- Students under 30 years old studying full-time can get a student discount on public transport.
- Discount: about 45% off the normal adult fare.
- Valid for metro, trams, buses, commuter trains, and ferries in the Helsinki region.
- Requirement: official student status verified via the   Tuudo app   or HSL card linked to student ID.
- Practical tip: always carry your student ID when traveling.

HOAS (Foundation for Student Housing in the Helsinki Region)
- Provides affordable student apartments in Helsinki, Espoo, Vantaa.
- Options: shared apartments, studios, one-bedroom flats, family apartments.
- Rent is significantly cheaper than private market housing.
- Apartments are furnished or unfurnished depending on type.
- Application: online via HOAS.fi, requires proof of student status.
- Waiting time: varies, but early application is recommended.

Student Union METKA
- Membership gives access to Zone Sports Services (gyms, classes, ball sports, yoga).
- Provides discounts on cultural events, restaurants, and shops.
- Organizes student parties, festivals, and tutoring programs.
- Digital student card included in membership.

Tuudo App
- Official student app for Metropolia.
- Functions:
  - Digital student ID (used for discounts).
  - Class schedules and exam timetables.
  - Campus maps and cafeteria menus.
- Student ID in Tuudo is accepted by HSL, cafeterias, and many shops.

Other Discounts & Perks
-   Kela benefits: housing allowance and meal subsidy for eligible students.
-   Cultural discounts: museums, theaters, concerts often have student tickets.
-   Public services: libraries, sports centers, and swimming pools offer reduced student fees.
-   Events: student festivals (Vappu, sitsit, fuksiaiset) often include free or discounted entry.

Summary
Metropolia students benefit from HSL transport discounts, affordable housing via HOAS, subsidized meals, and wide-ranging cultural and sports discounts. The Tuudo app and METKA student union membership are key tools to access these perks.
