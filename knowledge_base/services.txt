Student Services at Metropolia UAS

 Library
- Libraries on 4 campuses + Haaga-Helia & Laurea.
- MetCat Finna system for search & reservations.
- E-resources and LibGuides.

IT Services
- Student accounts with username & password.
- Helpdesk: hd.metropolia.fi, helpdesk@metropolia.fi.
- Software: Microsoft Azure, VMware.
- Platforms: Moodle, OMA, Tuudo.

 Health & Wellbeing
- Adult Health Clinic, counseling.
- FSHS healthcare for degree students.
- Support for harassment cases.

 Sports
- Zone Sports Services: gyms, classes, ball sports, yoga.
- Access via METKA membership.

 Cafeterias & Discounts
- Student-priced meals on campus.
- Tuudo app student ID for discounts.

 Housing
- HOAS apartments: shared, studio, family.
- Metropolia does not provide housing directly.

 Student Union (METKA)
- Advocacy, events, tutoring.
- Membership includes sports & discounts.
