 Academic Year, Holidays, and Internship at Metropolia UAS (2025–2026)

 Academic Year
- Autumn semester: August 20 – December 12, 2025.
- Spring semester: January 7 – May 15, 2026.
- Exams: held at the end of each semester.
- Format: lectures, seminars, laboratory work, and project-based learning.

 Holidays
- Winter break: late December – early January (between semesters).
- Spring break: usually in April (about one week, depending on the faculty).
- Summer break: June – August.

Internship in the Metropolis
- Mandatory part of studies.
- Scope: 30–120 ECTS credits depending on the program.
- Timing: usually during summer, but can also be scheduled during the academic year.
- Location: companies, public institutions, and research centers in Helsinki and other Finnish cities.
- Goals:
  - Develop professional skills.
  - Gain experience in real working environments.
  - Build contacts for future career opportunities.
- Outcome: internship report and evaluation, included in the final degree.
